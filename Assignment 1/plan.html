Goals

The purpose of CossiePlay is to provide customers creative and fancy cosplay costumes and to invite the users to attend a monthly cosplay party. The website is created in order to improve the sale by getting on the online market.

Success Evaluation

The site is provided with information that the client has provided. It is easy to navigate through the website, and the goal for the website is focused on the frontpage telling the people to sign up.The shop is clear and easy to read.

Target Audience

The target audience of CossiePlay is a specific group of teenagers that are fans of various characters e.g. From movies or videogames.
The site is therefore designed in order to fit the audience by having pictures of other fans at cosplay parties so they can relate. It is provided clear information about what the possibilities for CossiePlay are.
